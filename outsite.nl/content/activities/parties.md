description_en: 
  "Every few months we have a big themed queer party at our bar. The bar (and visitors!) gets dressed up in a theme, (some of our favourite recent themes are Super Smash Hoes 🎮, Between Cunt & Kitsch 💅, Halos & Horns 😈, and Cunty Country Chaos 🤠), complete with fun cocktails, DJs, decorations, music, lighting, etc. These parties are organized by the OUTnight committee who is responsible for all the brainstorming and preparation to ensure a banger party 🔥. It’s super fun to work with this committee to create a super fun night attracting regular visitors as well as new people!"

Het is super gaaf om met je commissie een fabuleus feest neer te zetten waar zowel vaste bezoekers als nieuwe mensen komen. En het zijn ook gewoon hele gezellige avonden om langs te komen!"
